Tools & Apps

Coastal Eng. HydroCalc

Wave length from period and depth via linear dispersion. Classifies wave theory using the Le Méhaut (1976) applicability diagram.
Offshore Eng. Wave Slamming Uncertainty

Probabilistic estimation of the slamming coefficient Cs for breaking waves on offshore monopiles. Combines Wienke-Oumeraci theory with Bayesian and frequentist meta-analysis of 15 published sources.
Solar PV Bird Dropping AEP Impact

Quantifies annual energy production loss from bird dropping soiling on solar PV farms. Literature review of 11 studies plus PVGIS-based calculator.
Utility Nemlig Price Finder

Parses a shopping list and searches Nemlig.com for the cheapest matching products. Supports Cookidoo list formatting.
Game Sudoku Technique Trainer

Trains one solving technique at a time through a structured curriculum. Includes hints, pencil marks, undo, and progress tracking.
